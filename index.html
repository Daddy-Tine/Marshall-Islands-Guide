About the Marshall Islands

The Republic of the Marshall Islands (RMI) is a sovereign nation in the central Pacific Ocean, comprising 29 atolls and five islands spread across 750,000 square miles of ocean. Known for its stunning coral atolls, rich marine life, and vibrant culture, the RMI offers a unique blend of natural beauty and economic opportunities. The economy is driven by agriculture, tourism, and offshore financial services, with major hubs in Majuro and Kwajalein. English and Marshallese are the official languages, and the US dollar is the official currency.
